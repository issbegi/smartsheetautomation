Case Studies

At SmartSheet Automation, we've helped businesses across various industries streamline workflows, save time, and reduce errors through our custom automation solutions. Here are some examples of the real-world results we've achieved for our clients.

Automating Reconciliations for a Financial Institution

Problem:

Manual reconciliation processes were time-consuming and error-prone, requiring significant staff hours and creating compliance risks.

Solution:

Built an automated Excel tool using VBA and Power Query to streamline the reconciliation process, automating data extraction, comparison, and exception highlighting.

Outcome:

Reduced reconciliation time by 70%, significantly improved accuracy, and ensured consistent compliance with financial regulations. Staff were freed up to focus on resolving exceptions rather than finding them.

Excel-Based Report Automation for a Bank

Problem:

The bank's reporting process involved extensive manual data entry and formatting, taking valuable time away from analysis and decision-making.

Solution:

Created a macro-driven Excel solution to automatically generate reports by pulling data from multiple sources, formatting it consistently, and creating visualizations.

Outcome:

Saved several hours per week of manual work, allowing teams to focus on analysis rather than data preparation. Improved reporting accuracy and consistency across departments.
