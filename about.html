About SmartSheet Automation

Your Trusted Partner for Business Process Automation

Who We Are

SmartSheet Automation is a leading business process automation firm specializing in Excel, Power Query, VBA, and Power BI solutions. We help businesses streamline workflows, eliminate manual tasks, and enhance data-driven decision-making through custom automation tools.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with cutting-edge automation solutions that boost efficiency, reduce errors, and save time.

Our Vision

To be the go-to automation partner for businesses seeking to maximize productivity using Excel, Power Query, and Power BI.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expertise in Automation – 10+ years of experience in Excel, Power Query, VBA, and Power BI.
  • Custom Solutions – Tailored automation tools designed to meet your specific business needs.
  • Time & Cost Savings – Our solutions drastically reduce manual work and improve efficiency.
  • Proven Track Record – Successful automation projects across banking, retail, finance, and logistics.

Founded by Patrick Kobe, SmartSheet Automation started as a passion for simplifying business processes. Over the years, we have helped multiple businesses transform their workflows, making manual, repetitive tasks a thing of the past.
